About NepalQuiz.xyz

NepalQuiz.xyz is a dedicated platform designed for students, learners, and aspirants preparing for Loksewa Aayog examinations and Nepal General Knowledge quizzes. Our mission is to make exam preparation easy, accessible, and effective through free online quizzes, solved old questions, study notes, and mock tests.

🎯 Our Vision

To become Nepal’s most trusted online platform for Loksewa preparation and quiz-based learning, helping thousands of aspirants achieve their dream of success in competitive exams.

📘 What We Offer

  • ✅ Loksewa Aayog Preparation – Syllabus, solved papers, and subject-wise notes
  • ✅ Old Question Collection – Gorkhapatra questions with solutions
  • ✅ Online Quizzes – GK, Current Affairs, History, Geography, Constitution
  • ✅ Mock Tests – Section Officer, Nayab Subba, Kharidar, and more
  • ✅ Mobile & Desktop Friendly – Practice anytime, anywhere
